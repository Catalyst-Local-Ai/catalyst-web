Why Choose Catalyst-Local-AI?

Cloud-Free Privacy

Keep your data secure and private by running AI models entirely on your local machine. No need to worry about data leaving your network.

Forget complex dependencies. Our Docker-based setup gets you up and running in minutes, so you can focus on building, not configuring.

Powered by Ollama

2.

Start the Containers
